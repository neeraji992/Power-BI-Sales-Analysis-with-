# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Sales Performance Analysis** using business intelligence techniques to evaluate sales, profitability, regional performance, product categories, seasonal trends, and the impact of discounts. The objective is to identify business opportunities, optimize profitability, and support data-driven decision-making.

| Discount | Average Profit |

## 📌 Key Insights

---

# 🚀 Business Optimization Opportunities

## 1️⃣ Reduce High Discounts

- Avoid discounts above **30%**, except for inventory clearance.
- Implement approval workflows for high-discount transactions.

---

## 2️⃣ Improve Furniture Margins

- Review supplier pricing.
- Optimize logistics costs.
- Increase product prices where feasible.
- Promote bundled offerings instead of heavy discounts.

---

## 3️⃣ Improve Loss-Making Products

Focus on **Tables**, which generate high sales but negative profit.

Recommended actions:

- Review procurement costs.
- Optimize pricing strategy.
- Reduce unnecessary discounting.
- Negotiate supplier contracts.

---

## 4️⃣ Expand High-Profit Categories

Increase investment in:

- Technology
- Office Supplies

through marketing campaigns, inventory expansion, and cross-selling strategies.

---

## 5️⃣ Regional Growth Strategy

- Replicate West region's successful sales strategies.
- Increase marketing efforts in South.
- Improve profitability in Central through cost optimization.

---

## 6️⃣ Prepare for Seasonal Demand

Increase inventory, staffing, and marketing investment before **Q4 (September–December)** to maximize seasonal sales.

---

## 7️⃣ Customer Segmentation

- Analyze discount behavior across customer segments.
- Offer personalized promotions instead of blanket discounts.
- Improve customer lifetime value through targeted marketing.

---

## 8️⃣ Profit-Focused KPIs to Monitor

- Profit Margin %
- Discount %
- Sales per Order
- Profit per Order
- Average Selling Price
- Region-wise Profit Margin
- Category-wise Return on Sales
- Loss-Making Product Count

---

# 🎯 Business Conclusion

The business demonstrates strong year-over-year growth, driven primarily by the **Technology** category and the **West** region.

However, profitability is negatively impacted by:

- Excessive discounting
- Low-margin Furniture products
- Loss-making Tables sub-category

By optimizing discount policies, improving Furniture margins, strengthening high-profit categories, and preparing strategically for seasonal demand, the business can achieve sustainable revenue growth while significantly improving overall profitability.
