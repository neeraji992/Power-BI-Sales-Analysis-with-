Business Optimization Opportunities
1. Reduce High Discounts
•	Avoid discounts above 30% except for inventory clearance. 
•	Implement approval workflows for large discounts. 
2. Improve Furniture Margins
•	Review supplier pricing. 
•	Increase prices selectively. 
•	Reduce logistics costs. 
•	Bundle products instead of offering steep discounts. 
3. Fix Loss-Making Products
•	Investigate Tables, which generate high sales but negative profit. 
•	Review pricing, procurement costs, and discount strategy. 
4. Expand High-Profit Categories
•	Invest more in Technology and Office Supplies through marketing and inventory allocation. 
5. Focus on Regional Growth
•	Scale successful practices from the West region. 
•	Run targeted campaigns to improve sales in the South and margins in the Central region. 
6. Prepare for Seasonal Demand
•	Increase inventory, staffing, and marketing ahead of Q4 (September–December) to capitalize on peak demand. 
7. Customer Segmentation
•	Analyze discount usage by customer segment. 
•	Offer personalized promotions instead of blanket discounts. 
8. Profit-Focused KPIs
Monitor these regularly:
•	Profit Margin % 
•	Discount % 
•	Sales per Order 
•	Profit per Order 
•	Average Selling Price 
•	Region-wise Profit Margin 
•	Category-wise Return on Sales 
•	Loss-making Product Count 
Overall Business Conclusion
The business shows strong year-over-year growth, driven primarily by the Technology category and the West region. The biggest profitability challenge is excessive discounting and low-margin Furniture products—particularly Tables. By tightening discount policies, optimizing underperforming products, and investing further in high-margin categories, the business can improve profitability while sustaining sales growth.
